# Estate-Management-Application
A web application for easy management of estates, buildings and occupants all in one place.

INSTALLATION

Import the "estate.sql" file inside the database folder to your database setup. Ensure that the database name is "estate".

There are 3 major users of the application
1. Main admin who is also refered to as the government official in charge of estate management. He creates estates in the system and assigns managers who are refered as "Estate Admins" to overseer the affairs in the estates and ensure the necessary levies and legal obligation of the estates are properly met.

2. Estate Admin: this user is in charge of managing all the buildings in an estate and accept occupants into the estate buildings. He ensures that occupants of the buildings in an estate are well managed and complaints attended to. He also maintains the owners of the estate buildings and ensure appropriate transfer of building ownership in case of sales of estate buildings.

3. Occupants/Tenants: these are the people living in the estate buildings. they get registered into the system by an estate admin when they have fully rented an apartment in the estate. Fully registered occupant can have access to his/her dashboard from where he/she can make payment, make complaints and perform other actions.
